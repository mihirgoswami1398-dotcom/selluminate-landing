Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Selluminate ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Selluminate Shopify application and website at selluminate.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you install and use Selluminate, we collect the following information:

  • Shopify store information: Your store domain, store name, and access credentials necessary to connect to the Shopify API.
  • Product images: Photos you upload to generate product listings. These are temporarily stored on Cloudinary for processing.
  • Generated listings: The AI-generated product listing content we create for you, stored in our database to power the History feature.
  • Usage data: Number of listings generated, timestamps, and feature usage to manage your plan limits.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Selluminate service — generating product listings from your photos.
  • To publish products to your Shopify store on your behalf when you click Publish.
  • To maintain your generation history so you can access past listings.
  • To track usage against your plan limits.
  • To improve our AI models and service quality.

3. Image Storage

Product images you upload are sent to Cloudinary for temporary storage and processing. Images are used solely to generate your product listings and are subject to a 30-day retention policy after which they may be deleted. We do not use your product images for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Selluminate:

  • Shopify: To connect to your store and publish products.
  • Google Vertex AI (Gemini): To analyze product images and generate listing content.
  • Cloudinary: For temporary image storage and processing.
  • Firebase: For user authentication and data storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide our service. We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS. Access tokens for your Shopify store are stored securely and never exposed publicly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your generated listing data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Product images on Cloudinary are subject to a 30-day automatic deletion policy.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Shopify App Specific

When you install Selluminate through the Shopify App Store, you grant us permission to access your store's product data as specified during installation. You can revoke this access at any time by uninstalling the app from your Shopify admin.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
